Cloud Atlas

Rating: 7.5/10 Year: 2015 Director: Wachowski, Tom Tykwer

Description of Cloud Atlas

Cloud Atlas is a glittering compendium of interlacing parables. Divided into six different accounts spanning several centuries, Mitchell ranges from the journal of a 19th-century American notary to the post-apocalyptic memoir of a herdsman, Zachry. Each testament breaches time and space.
